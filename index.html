Puiforcat Cannes Five-Piece Place Setting

Year: 1928

Exquisitely fluted handles and ring detailing characterize this signature Puiforcat pattern. Renowned for the fine symmetry of its design and the solid, perfect balance of its forms, this collection was chosen by Jean Puiforcat for his own wedding.

Sterling silver. Includes dinner fork, dinner knife, dessert spoon, salad fork and tea spoon.

Puiforcat Cardinal Five-Piece Place Setting

Year: 1925

Produced from a 17th-century drawing, this is a pattern of simple forms and classic grace. This setting’s time-honored roots can be seen in such elements as a the reinforced center tine of the fork and a knife design that recalls the curve-bladed style of old hunting knives.

Silverplate. Includes dinner fork, dinner knife, dessert spoon, salad fork and tea spoon. Also available in sterling silver as the Richelieu pattern.

$540.00

Year: 1924

In this classic pattern, the clean, modern shape of the utensils is balanced by the fluted handles’ complex interplay of light and shadow.

Silverplate. Includes dinner fork, dinner knife, dessert spoon, salad fork and tea spoon.
